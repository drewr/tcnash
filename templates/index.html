Trinity Church is a church plant partnering with Grace Community Church for ministry in Nashville's city-center. With an official launch set for mid-September 2010, we currently meet for Bible study and prayer on Sunday evenings at the Sevier Park Community Center in the 12th South neighborhood of Nashville. The meetings begin at 5:30PM and last until approximately 7:00PM. Childcare is provided. Audio recordings are available.
