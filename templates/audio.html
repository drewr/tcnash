Sevier Park Sessions

Between February and September of 2010, a group of approximately thirty people met weekly at a local community center to discuss why Nashville may need another church. By the conclusion of their time together, it became clear that Jesus Christ built his ministry on the expansion of his church. The news of his life, death, and resurrection is best demonstrated by people committing to live close to one another in self-sacrificial, counter-cultural love. Each of these sessions was recorded and is available here.
