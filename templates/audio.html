Sevier Park Sessions

Sunday, 16 May 2010
The Church's Mission: Acts 1:6-11
Matt McCullough — MP3
Using Jesus' commission to his followers in Acts 1, we unpack how the church exists to witness to Jesus through both its clear verbal testimony and through the quality of its life as community.

Sunday, 23 May 2010
The Church's Message: Acts 2, 13, 17
Matt McCullough — MP3
The church exists to witness to Jesus, which means clearly explaining what he is like and why his life and work matter. We look at several of the apostles' speeches to show the content of their message about Jesus.

Sunday, 6 June 2010
The Church's Life Together: Acts 2:42-47
Matt McCullough — MP3
Witnessing to Jesus also involves the church living as a distinct community that illustrates his character. Luke summarizes how members of the first church shared all of life together.

Sunday, 20 June 2010
The Gospel and Trinity Church: An Overview
Matt McCullough — MP3
This series introduction answers two simple questions: What is the gospel? And why does the gospel matter for church life?

Sunday, 27 June 2010
The Gospel and Trinity Church: The Gospel and Community
Matt McCullough — MP3
Strong community, we believe, is essential for healthy, God-honoring church life. The gospel both creates and controls healthy community.

Sunday, 18 July 2010
The Gospel and Trinity Church: Worship
Matt McCullough — MP3
What does the gospel have to do with our worship? The gospel makes all worship possible, and it sets the content for our corporate worship services as we respond together to what God has said and done. We also discuss this and how worship will look at Trinity.

Sunday, 25 July 2010
The Gospel and Trinity Church: Prayer
Matt McCullough — MP3
Our goal as individual believers and as a corporate body is to engage the power that brought Jesus from the grave. Because that power is God's and not ours, the only tool available to us is prayer. The gospel tells us why we pray and what to pray.

Sunday, 1 August 2010
The Gospel and Trinity Church: Mission
Matt McCullough — MP3
The church's mission is both to explain the gospel and illustrate the gospel.
